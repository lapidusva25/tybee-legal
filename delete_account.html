Delete Your TyBee Account

Last updated: June 24, 2026 · TyBee mobile app

You can ask us to delete your TyBee account and the personal data associated with it at any time. Here's how.

How to request deletion

Email val.lapidus@gmail.com from (or mentioning) the email address on your TyBee account, with the subject "Delete my TyBee account."

We will verify the request comes from the account owner and then delete the account. We aim to complete deletions within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in credentials.
  • Your profile information (name and email address).
  • Data tied to your account, such as your listening history and your record of accepting the Terms of Use.

What may be retained

We may retain information that is not linked to you, such as anonymous, aggregated data about places that have little or no content (used to improve coverage), and any records we are required to keep for legal or security reasons. None of this identifies you.

Contact

Questions about account deletion: val.lapidus@gmail.com.
