TyBee — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026 · Applies to the TyBee mobile app (beta)

TyBee is a location-based audio tour app: as you walk or drive, it finds nearby points of interest and plays short AI-narrated audio about your surroundings. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, and how it is handled. TyBee is currently a free, invite-only beta operated by an individual developer ("we", "us").

If you have questions, contact us at val.lapidus@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information. To use TyBee you create an account. We collect your email address and the name you provide. You can sign in with an email and password or with Google Sign-In; in either case we receive your email address (and, from Google, your basic profile name). Authentication is handled by our provider, Supabase.
  • Location. With your permission, the app uses your device's location while you are using it (foreground) to find points of interest near you. Your location is sent to our server in real time to build your queue of nearby places. We do not store your location history or link your location to your identity. The only location data we retain is anonymous and not tied to you: the coordinates of spots where no content was found yet, which we use to improve coverage.
  • Diagnostic and feedback data. If you use the in-app "Log an issue" feature, your report (subject, description, your account email, and a technical debug log) is emailed to our support address so we can troubleshoot. The debug log may include recent technical details such as approximate location, timing, and device performance data from your current session.
  • Usage data. We may collect basic, app-interaction events (such as opening the app or starting and finishing a narration) to understand how TyBee is used and to improve it.
  • Terms acceptance. When you accept the beta Terms of Use, we record which version you accepted and when, and email you a copy.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core service — find nearby places and generate and play narration.
  • To create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • To respond to issues you report and fix bugs.
  • To understand usage and improve the app's quality, coverage, and performance.
  • To communicate with you about the beta (for example, the Terms copy email).

How content is generated

Narration is created by sending publicly available source text (from Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects) to AI services that translate, summarize, and convert it to speech. We do not send your personal information to those services as part of generating narration.

Service providers we share data with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the service providers that operate TyBee, and only as needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — account authentication and database hosting.
  • Render — hosting for our application server.
  • Google Cloud (Text-to-Speech) and Anthropic (Claude) — generating narration from public source text.
  • Google — if you choose Google Sign-In.

Advertising

During the beta we may test advertising, so you might hear or see ads. If and when we do, advertising partners may collect information such as device or advertising identifiers to deliver and measure ads. We will update this policy and our Play Store data disclosures before any such advertising is enabled.

Data retention and your choices

  • This is a beta. We may reset settings, delete data, or remove accounts at any time, as described in the Terms of Use.
  • You can request that we delete your account and associated personal information by emailing val.lapidus@gmail.com.
  • You can revoke the app's location permission at any time in your device settings; TyBee will fall back to a basic radius search and some features may not work.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections to our servers and authentication tokens managed by our provider. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

TyBee is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it. (Features intended for younger listeners are not enabled in this beta.)

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will revise the "Last updated" date above, and for significant changes we will provide a more prominent notice.

Contact
